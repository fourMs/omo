Sound Saber

Turn Audio on, then hold the pad and swing the phone like a saber rod. Allow motion (and orientation on iPhone).

Speed → loudness · tilt height → filter · horizontal / vertical swing → twin delay lines · pointing → ring modulation.

Based on the fourMs MoCap-Synthesiser fSM.soundsaber patch (pulse, delays, ring mod, bandpass). Research: Nymoen et al., NIME 2011 — URN:NBN:no-29584.
