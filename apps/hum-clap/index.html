Hum Clap

Teach then play

1. Turn Audio on and allow the mic.

2. Teach — hold each button (don’t tap): hum steadily, clap 2–3 times, then stay silent.

3. Listen — the app guesses hum / clap / silent and plays a different sound for each (tone · clap burst · quiet).

Need at least 5 examples per class. If everything sounds the same, tap Clear and teach again with louder, clearer gestures.

Teach: hold Hum → hold Clap → hold Silent (5+ each) → Listen
